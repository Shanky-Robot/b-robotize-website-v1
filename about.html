About Us

Our Company

B Robotize, under the leadership of Mr. Sankarshan B, drives innovation in robotics and automation. We specialize in research, development, and training across Robotics, AI, IoT, and Automation, empowering the next generation through STEM education and delivering cutting-edge industrial solutions. Our commitment is to democratize technology, making robotics and AI accessible through S.T.E.A.M. products, comprehensive learning labs, and tailored solutions for diverse industries.

Our team consists of experts in robotics, AI, and education, who are passionate about pushing the boundaries of technology and making it accessible to all.

"Our vision at B Robotize is to make robotics accessible to all. We're committed to elevating STEM education globally and empowering the next generation of innovators."

- Mr. Sankarshan B, Founder

Our Founder

Mr. Sankarshan B is the visionary behind B Robotize. He is a Robotics Engineer with 10+ Years Industry Experience with M.Tech in Industrial Automation & Robotics, B.Tech in Mechatronics, Dip. Electrical Engineering. He recognized the need for accessible robotics and AI education. He founded B Robotize with the goal of democratizing technology and empowering individuals to explore the exciting world of robotics and AI.

Mr. Sankarshan B is committed to fostering innovation and providing high-quality educational resources to inspire the next generation of engineers and technologists.
